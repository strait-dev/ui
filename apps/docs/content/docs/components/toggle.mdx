## Examples

### Emphasis

`default` renders a transparent button that fills on press; `outline` adds a border, making the boundary visible at rest.

<ComponentPreview name="toggle/emphasis" />

### Variants

Five semantic colour treatments for the pressed state: `default`, `destructive`, `success`, `info`, and `warning`.

<ComponentPreview name="toggle/variants" />

### Sizes

A contiguous scale from `xs` (24 px) through `xl` (40 px).
