## Examples

### Variants

Four surface styles control how the row sits against its background: `default` (transparent), `outline` (bordered), `muted` (subtle fill), and `ghost` (only tints on hover).

<ComponentPreview name="item/variants" />

### Sizes

A contiguous size scale from `xs` to `xl` adjusts padding, gap, and text proportionally.

<ComponentPreview name="item/sizes" />

### With actions and badges

Compose `ItemActions` with badges and buttons to surface status and contextual controls on the trailing edge.
